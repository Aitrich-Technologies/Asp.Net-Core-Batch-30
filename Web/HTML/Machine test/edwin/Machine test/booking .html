Terms and Conditions:
Booking and payment

 Contractual Agreement: Clearly state that a contract exists once the agency confirms the booking and receives a deposit.
Deposit and Balance Payments: Define the required deposit amount and the due date for the remaining balance.

Cancellation and refunds

 Cancellation Policy: Provide a clear cancellation policy with specific charges based on the timing of cancellation.
No-Show/Early Departure: Address consequences for no-shows or early departures, potentially resulting in no refund for unused services.

Liabilities and limitations

Disclaimer: Clearly state that the agency acts as an agent for third-party providers (hotels, airlines, etc.) and is not responsible for their actions or failures.
Limitation of Liability: Define the extent of the agency's liability, often limited to the total amount paid for the tour and excluding consequential damages or additional expenses.
