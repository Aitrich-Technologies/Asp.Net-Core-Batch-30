About Us

FotMob is a leading mobile application that has revolutionized how football (soccer) fans follow the sport. Launched in 2004 by brothers Christer and Tommy Nordvik in Norway, it originated from their personal frustration with the lack of reliable and comprehensive ways to track matches from afar. Since then, it has grown into an indispensable tool for over 20 million users globally, offering real-time live scores, lightning-fast goal notifications, and extensive statistics for more than 400 competitions worldwide. Users can customize alerts for their favorite teams and players, access in-depth match data including Expected Goals (xG) and shot maps, and even view official match highlights or listen to audio commentary. FotMob's success is attributed to its vast data coverage, user-friendly interface, and commitment to delivering timely and accurate information, effectively "changing the way the world follows football."

FotMob is a highly popular and widely trusted mobile application for football (soccer) fans worldwide. It's designed to keep users updated with real-time information about football matches from hundreds of leagues and competitions across the globe. Here's a breakdown of what "in FotMob" generally means and its key features: What FotMob provides: * Live Scores and Instant Notifications: This is a core feature. You get immediate updates on goals, red cards, penalties, and other key match events, often faster than watching a broadcast. * Detailed Match Statistics: FotMob offers in-depth stats for matches, including: * Expected Goals (xG) and Expected Assists (xA) * Shot maps * Possession percentages * Shots on target/off target * Fouls, corners, offsides * Player ratings (often a unique feature that fans appreciate) * Lineups and formations * Personalized News and Alerts: You can customize the app to follow your favorite teams, leagues, and players. This ensures you only receive news and alerts relevant to your interests. * Live Text Commentary: For matches you can't watch, FotMob provides minute-by-minute text commentary, keeping you informed of the action as it unfolds. * Transfer News and Breaking Stories: The app aggregates news from various sources, including transfer rumors, completed deals, injury updates, and other significant football news. * Match Highlights and Audio Commentary: For some matches, FotMob provides official video highlights and/or audio commentary. * League Tables, Fixtures, and Results: Comprehensive overviews of league standings, upcoming matches, and past results are readily available. * Player Profiles and Performance Metrics: You can delve into individual player stats, career history, and performance metrics. * TV Schedules: For many competitions, FotMob provides information on where and when to watch matches. * User-Friendly Interface: The app is generally praised for its clean, intuitive design, making it easy to navigate and find the information you need. Why people use FotMob: * Real-time updates: For fans who can't always watch games live, FotMob offers a fantastic way to stay connected. * Comprehensive coverage: It covers a vast number of leagues and competitions, from major international tournaments to smaller domestic leagues. * Rich data: The detailed statistics provide valuable insights for casual fans and those interested in in-depth analysis. * Customization: The ability to personalize alerts and news feeds makes it a tailored experience. * Reliability: FotMob is known for its fast and accurate data delivery. In summary, "in FotMob" refers to the rich and detailed football information available within the FotMob application, serving as a go-to platform for millions of football enthusiasts.
